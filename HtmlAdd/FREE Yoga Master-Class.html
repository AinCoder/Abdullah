 3 Little-Known Facts to Consider Before 
Becoming a Certified Yoga Teacher  

Choose wisely your Yoga Style 

Sounds like a no-brainer, doesn't it? But yet, you would be surprised by how many people who tries to bypass this phase.

There are many yoga styles but for the sake of this our first meeting Ill only mention the 4 main ones:

  • Hatha Yoga : All Inclusive
  • Ashtanga Yoga : For the Dynamic
  • Kundalini Yoga : For the Spiritual
  • Yin Yoga : For the Relax One

Find a Registered Yoga School

Yoga studios use Yoga Alliance certification as a baseline to gauge the competency and quality of teachers before employing them.

So, if you're torn between having or not having a certificate, I'd recommend grabbing it with both hands as this simple document will serve as a master key thatll open so many doors for you...

..And the best part is that you can get certified as a yoga teacher in only 30 days or at your own pace from any place in the world

This is definitely the fastest way I know to BECOME a Certified Yoga Teacher & start doing what you truly love and enjoy.

Yoga Teacher your NEW Career.

Most of Aspiring Yoga Teachers assume that they cannot make a living just by teaching yoga, and think they need to have a real job along with it. 

For beginner teachers, its recommended to work for an already existing yoga Studio to gain practical experience but to also create a name for yourself and have a fix salary to pay for your basic needs..

...And a time will come when youll be ready to break out on your own and open your own studio or Yoga Center where you can organize retreats and offer your own PERSONALIZED AND SIGNATURE PROGRAMS to take your career to the next level
